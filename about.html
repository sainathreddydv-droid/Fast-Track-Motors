Our story

A garage built on straight talk.

We're a small, independent team in Newcastle who got tired of seeing drivers handed bills they didn't understand. So we built the garage we'd want to use ourselves.

Why we started

Honest mechanics, plain English.

Fast Track Motors began with a simple frustration: too many garages treat customers like they won't understand. We do the opposite. We show you the worn part, explain what it does, and let you make the call.

Every technician here is qualified and insured, every repair is guaranteed for 12 months, and every price is agreed before a spanner is lifted. That's it. No mystery, no upsell, no surprises on the invoice.

What we stand for

Three things we never compromise on.

Honesty

We only recommend what your car genuinely needs. If it can wait, we'll tell you. If it's urgent, we'll explain why.

Quality

Genuine or OE-matching parts, qualified hands, and a 12-month guarantee on every repair we carry out.

Speed

Most jobs done same day. We respect that you've got a life to get on with — and a car you need back.
