MOT questions

Everything you need to know.

When is my MOT due?

Your MOT is due every year, on the anniversary of your last test (or three years after a new car's registration). You can check the exact date free on the GOV.UK website using your registration — or just call us and we'll look it up for you.

Can I drive without a valid MOT?

Only to a pre-booked MOT appointment. Driving without a valid MOT otherwise can mean a fine of up to £1,000 and invalidated insurance. If yours is due, book in early to be safe.

What happens if my car fails?

We'll explain exactly what failed and why, and give you a clear, no-pressure quote to put it right. Get the work done with us within 10 days and the retest is free.

How long does an MOT take?

The test itself takes around 45–60 minutes. You're welcome to wait in our reception, or drop the car off and we'll call you the moment it's ready.

Can I get my MOT and service together?

Absolutely — and it's the smart way to do it. Booking both together saves you a trip and often money. Just choose "MOT + Service" when you book online.
